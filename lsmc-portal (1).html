POLICIES & CONDUCT

01
Professional Conduct & Respect
▶

All staff are expected to maintain professional conduct both in-game and out-of-game at all times.

  • Respect is mandatory toward all members, patients, and visitors.
  • Harassment, discrimination, or verbal abuse will not be tolerated.
  • Conflicts must be resolved through official channels (Discord tickets).
  • Off-duty conduct reflects on the LSMC — act accordingly.
02
Roleplay Standards (RP)
▶

All interactions must remain immersive and coherent within the GTA RP framework.

  • No Power Gaming — forced unrealistic actions are prohibited.
  • No Meta Gaming — never use out-of-character information in-character.
  • Fear RP applies at all times — your character values their life.
  • Medical staff must prioritize patient care over personal engagements.
03
Medical Operations & Protocol
▶

LSMC operates under standardized medical RP protocols to ensure quality and consistency.

  • All patient interactions must follow triage priority (critical → stable).
  • Hospital grounds are safe zones — no violent engagements on hospital property.
  • EMS may operate in combat zones only with law enforcement escort.
  • Document significant medical events in the shift report channel.
04
Equipment & Uniform Policy
▶

Proper identification and uniform must be worn during all active duty shifts.

  • LSMC-issued uniforms must be worn while on active duty.
  • Department vehicles are for official use only — no personal use.
  • Medical equipment must be returned to the inventory after each shift.
  • Staff ID badges must be visible at all times within hospital grounds.
05
Disciplinary Procedures
▶

Violations of LSMC policy are subject to progressive disciplinary action.

  • 1st offense: Written warning issued by a supervisor.
  • 2nd offense: Temporary suspension (24h – 72h).
  • 3rd offense: Permanent termination from LSMC roster.
  • Severe violations (cheating, doxxing, RDM) result in immediate removal.
